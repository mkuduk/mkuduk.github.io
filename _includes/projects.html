Modeling Gene Expression with Differential Equations

  • Mathematical Modeling

In this paper I examine a system of first order differential equations as a model for gene expression. I solve the system for equilibrium points and analyze the points through eigenvalues and bifircations in order to understand the biological relevance. Julia is used to plot solutions for analysis.

DEHP Research

  • Data Analysis

I conducted research on Di(2-ethylhexyl)phthalate, a chemical added to some plastics to make them flexible, using data from the SRA database. Through analysis of this data I was able to target genes of interest that are impacted negatively in their expression due to exposure of DEHP.
